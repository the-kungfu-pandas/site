Ideal Jobs - Industry Data

Each team member in our group have different ideal jobs.
sA commonality amongst our jobs is that they are all derivatives of the IT Industry, with emphasis on design, development and management; whilst integrating aspects of business and security.

Ideal Jobs

Chloe - UX Design

It involves planning and create new aspects of software that are easy to learn/understand, are memorable, pleasing to use for the user, and maintain a certain level of creativity and quality that ties in with existing elements of the software. A UX designer is in charge of monitoring and collaborating with teams to create the best user experience possible for users of the software, and is continuously thinking about how the users’ experiences can be improved through the creation of journey maps, flows and prototypes.

Mike - Sales Engineer

Whereas, a sales Engineer focuses on interacting with the company directly itself, this roles focuses on “closing the deals”, showcasing presentation and swaying clients in participating in the company. This job involves a high understanding of cyber security.

James - Cloud Architect

A cloud architect is someone who specialises in analysing a business’ cloud computing strategy through aspects such as design, strategies, management and monitor the cloud system. A cloud architect also manages application architecture and deployment in cloud environments.

Long - Software Developer

Software Developers are the brains of the entire development process for a software program; basically, we analyze user's need and then write, test the software the meet those needs. We usually work closely with software programmers since we are the one who design the program and give instructions to the programmers, who makes up software program.

Hajera - Web Design

A Web designer is somebody who organises content for the Web. Web designer role is mostly linked to the styling and layout of pages with content, including text and images.

Ishita - Business Analyst

A business analyst job typically includes creating a detailed business analysis, outlining the problems and opportunities and coming up with a solution in order to benefit the company. Along with a strong sense of business handling and management, information technological skills such as software development and data management are the key requirements for the job.
